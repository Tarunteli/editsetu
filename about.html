About EditSetu

We're building the bridge between India's most talented video editors and the creators who need them most.

✦ What Is EditSetu?

The Platform Built
For Indian Creators

EditSetu (Edit + Setu, meaning "bridge" in Hindi) is India's first dedicated marketplace connecting content creators with professional video editors. We noticed that creators were wasting hours on WhatsApp groups and Instagram DMs just trying to find a reliable editor — and we built a better solution.

Whether you create YouTube vlogs, Instagram Reels, wedding films, gaming montages, or motion graphics projects — EditSetu has verified editors who specialize in exactly what you need.

Every editor on our platform is manually reviewed. We check their portfolio, verify their experience, and only list professionals who consistently deliver quality work.

Why EditSetu Exists
  • Creators spend 5+ hours finding an editor — we make it 5 minutes.
  • Quality editors have no dedicated platform to showcase their work.
  • Platforms like Fiverr charge 20%+ in commissions — we charge zero.
  • Unverified editors lead to wasted time and money for creators.
  • Direct communication leads to better, faster creative collaboration.

EditSetu is free for both creators and editors. We believe the best creative partnerships shouldn't cost extra.
